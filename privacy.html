Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kelimenator mobile application collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

Kelimenator does not collect any personal data from users.

  • Does not request name, surname, or email
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not use camera, microphone, or similar permissions

However, within the app:

  • Selected language preferences
  • Learned words

are stored only on the user's device.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

The app is provided for free and contains ads served by Google AdMob.

AdMob may:

  • Collect device information (such as device type, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Show personalized ads based on user interests

This data is processed by Google and is subject to their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Users can limit ad personalization through their device settings.

3. Use of Data

  • Providing word learning functionality
  • Displaying words via widget
  • Serving advertisements

4. Data Sharing

Kelimenator:

  • Does not sell user data
  • Does not share user data with third parties

However, Google AdMob may process data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on the device
  • All data is deleted when the app is uninstalled

6. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

  • Data is not transmitted outside the device (except for advertising services)
  • Data is stored securely

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on the app page.
